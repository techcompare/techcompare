TicketStub Account & Data Deletion

TicketStub respects your privacy and gives you the ability to permanently delete your account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to workwithme785@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete My TicketStub Account".
  3. Include the email address associated with your TicketStub account.
  4. We will verify your request and begin the deletion process.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Your TicketStub account.
  • Your email address.
  • Your ticket stubs.
  • Your uploaded photos.
  • Your notes.
  • Your tags.
  • Your saved locations.
  • Any other content associated with your account.

What Data May Be Retained?

We do not retain your personal data after your account has been deleted, except where retention is required by applicable law or to resolve security, fraud, or legal issues.

Deletion Time

Account deletion requests are typically completed within 30 days of successful verification.
