Account Deletion

Delete Your TicketStub Account

Last updated: July 10, 2026

You can permanently delete your TicketStub account and all data associated with it at any time. This page explains how to request deletion and exactly what happens to your data.

01How to Request Deletion

Send an email requesting deletion — from the same address you used to sign up.

  1. Open your email app and address a new message to workwithme785@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: TicketStub Account Deletion Request
  3. Send it from the email address registered on your TicketStub account (this is how we verify it's you)
Request Account Deletion →

02What Gets Deleted

Account & loginDELETED
Email & passwordDELETED
Photos & ticketsDELETED
Notes, dates, locationsDELETED
Support email threadRETAINED BRIEFLY

The support email thread confirming your request may be kept briefly for our own records before being cleared, but is not linked to any active account once deletion is complete.

03Timeframe

Deletion requests are handled manually and are typically completed within a few business days of verification. You'll receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been permanently removed from our systems, including from our backend provider, Supabase.

04Before You Request Deletion

Deletion is permanent — your tickets and photos cannot be recovered afterward. If you'd simply like to stop using TicketStub without losing your data, you can leave your account inactive instead of requesting deletion.
